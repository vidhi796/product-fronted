Ancient History

Discovered in 1783 by local shepherds, these caves were sacred to the ancient Luminari civilization who believed the crystals contained trapped starlight. Archaeologists have found ritual chambers dating back 3,000 years where priests conducted "light ceremonies".

Explorer's Notes

The caves maintain a constant 85% humidity. Proper footwear with grip is essential for the crystal floors. Flash photography is prohibited in the Hall of Echoes as it disturbs the cave guardians (a rare species of blind bats).
